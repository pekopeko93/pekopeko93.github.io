About Me

Get to know your Japanese teacher

Hi, I'm your Japanese teacher!

Welcome to Peko Peko Japanese! I'm passionate about helping people learn Japanese in a way that's fun, effective, and stress-free.

I believe that learning a language should feel like an adventure, not a chore. That's why I focus on making my lessons engaging and practical, so you can use what you learn in real conversations right away.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to level up your skills, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

My Teaching Philosophy

What makes my approach different

Patient & Supportive

Everyone learns at their own pace. I never rush students and always create a safe space where it's okay to make mistakes - that's how we learn!

Practical & Relevant

I focus on Japanese you'll actually use. From daily conversations to understanding your favorite anime, I make learning relevant to your life.

Structured & Fun

My lessons follow a clear path so you always know you're making progress, but I keep things fun with games, stories, and cultural insights along the way.

My Background

Why I became a Japanese teacher

I started teaching Japanese because I love seeing that moment when something "clicks" for a student. There's nothing more rewarding than watching someone go from "I can't do this" to having their first real conversation in Japanese.

Over the years, I've taught students from all over the world with different goals - from passing the JLPT to preparing for trips to Japan to understanding Japanese media without subtitles.

  • Experience teaching students of all levels
  • Specializing in conversational Japanese
  • JLPT preparation (N5 to N1)
  • Cultural context integrated into every lesson
  • YouTube channel with free learning resources
