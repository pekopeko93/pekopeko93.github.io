What I Teach

Lessons tailored to your level and goals

Beginner

Getting Started

Perfect for complete beginners. Learn hiragana, katakana, basic grammar, essential vocabulary, and simple conversations. You'll be introducing yourself in Japanese in no time!

You'll learn:

  • Hiragana & Katakana
  • Self-introductions
  • Basic grammar patterns
  • Everyday vocabulary
Intermediate

Building Confidence

Ready to go deeper? Expand your grammar, grow your vocabulary, and start having real conversations. We'll work on reading, listening, and expressing your thoughts more naturally.

You'll learn:

  • Complex grammar patterns
  • Kanji reading & writing
  • Conversational fluency
  • Cultural context
Advanced

Mastering Japanese

For students who want to reach fluency. We'll tackle advanced grammar, nuanced expressions, business Japanese, and help you sound more natural in everyday speech.

You'll learn:

  • Advanced grammar & expressions
  • Business Japanese & keigo
  • Nuanced vocabulary
  • Native-like fluency

Special Focus Lessons

Targeted lessons for specific goals

JLPT Preparation

Preparing for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test? I offer focused preparation for all levels from N5 to N1, with practice tests, grammar drills, and study strategies.

Conversational Japanese

Want to just talk? These lessons focus on building your speaking and listening skills through natural conversation, role-playing, and discussion topics you enjoy.

Travel Japanese

Planning a trip to Japan? Learn the essential phrases, cultural etiquette, and practical language you need to navigate Japan with confidence.
