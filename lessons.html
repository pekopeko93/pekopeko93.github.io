My Specialty: Natural Japanese

I help you move beyond textbook Japanese and into real, natural conversation

Most Popular

Conversation Lessons

This is what I do best. My conversation lessons are designed to help you speak Japanese the way native speakers actually talk — not the stiff, formal style you find in textbooks. We'll work on natural expressions, rhythm, and the kind of Japanese you hear in everyday life.

What we focus on:

  • Natural expressions & casual speech
  • How to sound less like a textbook
  • Real-life topics and discussion
  • Nuance, tone, and cultural context
Intermediate

Breaking Through the Plateau

You've learned the basics but feel stuck? This is where most of my students start. We'll push past the intermediate wall by building your confidence in real conversations and expanding the way you express yourself.

What we focus on:

  • Moving from "correct" to "natural"
  • Expanding your vocabulary naturally
  • Listening comprehension with real content
  • Expressing opinions and feelings fluently
Advanced

Sounding Like a Native

For advanced learners who want to refine their Japanese. We'll work on the subtle differences that separate "good Japanese" from "natural Japanese" — the kind of polish that only comes from practice with a native speaker.

What we focus on:

  • Subtle nuance & word choice
  • Keigo and register switching
  • Idiomatic expressions & slang
  • Debate, storytelling, and complex topics

Beginners Are Welcome Too

Starting from zero? I'll get you speaking from day one

Beginner Lessons

Even as a beginner, my goal is to get you speaking as soon as possible. We'll build your foundation with hiragana, basic grammar, and essential phrases — but always with conversation as the end goal.

My Approach

I don't just teach rules and vocabulary lists. From the very first lesson, we practice real dialogue so you build the habit of actually using Japanese, not just studying it.

Your Pace, Your Goals

Whether you want to prepare for a trip to Japan, connect with Japanese friends, or enjoy media without subtitles — I'll tailor our lessons to what matters to you.
